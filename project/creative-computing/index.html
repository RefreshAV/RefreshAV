Creative Computing

Creative Computing is a course developed by an international network of educators passionate about exposing kids to the computer sciences. Broken up into 7 units, this course uses the popular Scratch programming environment to teach kids the basics of coding. Scratch is used over the world by students to easily create animations and games while acting as a stepping stone to the more advanced world of computer programming.

We’re considering offering such a course in the Annapolis Valley. Tell us what you think!

Refresh Annapolis Valley

Refresh is a community of designers and developers working to refresh the creative, technical, and professional culture of New Media endeavors in the Annapolis Valley while promoting design, technology, usability, and standards.

Refresh meets the second Tuesday evening of each month in the Wolfville area. These are casual affairs with short, member-interest talks followed by an opportunity to mingle.

All are welcome!
